How not to do a dragon attack
The target is a max TH7, which is difficult, but should be possible using lvl 1 dragons. Lightning spells would be better than rage.
First mistake: waking the CC troops. This isnt necessary. And worse, because dragons move towards the CC troops when close, the CC actually helps steer the dragons into the middle.
Second mistake: dropping the dragons all in one place. There is a risk that a lucky shot from a wizard hits all the dragons.
But, most importantly this steers the dragons around the outside. Once the troops are gone, the dragons will flame the nearest buildings... the elix and gold collectors in front to start with.
With the first collectors gone the drags continue to the next nearest building - the collectors and stores to the sides. Just one dragon has gone inwards, and is about to be wiped out by the airdef.
The dragon on the right which went in is just blowing up. One of the dragons on the left nearing the airdef is nearly dead...
Next dragon dies, and both airdefs are shooting at the remaining dragon on the left. Only 3 dragons have attacked the defence so far.
The 2 dragons which went to the left airdef failed. 2 more are about to try, and these fail as well... All the dragons are wiped out as the attack one or two at a time. End result was... only just achieved the 1* for 50%.
