A good GoWi attack
This is the target and troops. This attack is basically a GoWiPe attack, but using witches to fight enemy CC, and a couple more with the pekkas. Attacker is TH10, but the same attack can be done by TH9 by just leaving out the second 2 witches.
The CC fight could also have been done the normal way, using archers and wizards.
In the first screenshot the enemy CC has just been woken up with a couple of barbs (he also had a hog in case)
Using the witches to fight CC is a neat way of losing few if any troops.
Once the enemy troops are distracted by the skeletons, wizards are added to kill them.
Enemy CC is dead - main attack begins.
First golem is at the top, just under the explosion. The survivors from the CC fight are following it.
Second golem follows at the bottom, with wizards behind it - who will clear away the barracks and collectors. The golems are spread out to the sides of the main attack to distract as many guns as possible.
Main attack will be between these. The wizards to both sides are clearing the distractions which might lead pekkas around the outside.
Deploying no wizards in the middle leaves the dark drill and other buildings standing - which the pekkas will aim at.
The pekkas are launched... and in this instance also a couple of witches. Heroes and wizards in CC are about to follow.
A couple of wall-breakers for the first wall, and a jump spell for the next 2...
Now all the collectors and barracks have been killed outside the base, the pekkas don't have much choice but to head into the middle.
And apart from a few wizards killed by the cannon at the bottom, everyone has followed the route into the middle.
Rage spells (and freeze spells!) boost the attack at the critical moment.
The result - TH is about to die and it will be 2*. There were too many guns surrounding that central area for the troops to have any chance of fighting their way out for 3*, but the TH kill was never in doubt.
Although this was TH10, exactly the same without the last 2 witches would have got 2* for TH9.
